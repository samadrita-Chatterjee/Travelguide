Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu & Kashmir (J&K) is the northern most state in India. It is surrounded on the north by Afghanistan and China, on the east by China, on the south by Himachal Pradesh and Punjab in India, and on the west by the North-West Frontier Province and the Punjab Province of Pakistan.

Trekking

Some beautiful places for Trekking in Kashmir are Kashmir great lakes,Tarsar Mansar Trek,Naranag Mahlish,Naranag Gangabal Trek,Kolahoi Glacier,Pahalgam,Kashmir Alpine Lakes.
